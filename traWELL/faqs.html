Visiting Sri Lanka

What does TRAWELL mean?
TRAWELL means that we concern about every aspect of your visit, including entertainment, reliability, and safety so that you TRAVEL WELL.
When is the best time to visit Sri Lanka?
Sri Lanka is a fantastic destination at any time of year! As a year-round location, it features two monsoons that occur at different times in opposite sections of the island. So, if you want to experience the greatest weather at the western and southern seaside resorts, visit between December and April. Even during the monsoon, though, bright, pleasant days are usual, with just a few night time showers to be expected.
What kind of accommodation is found in Sri Lanka?
Sri Lanka has every form of accommodation imaginable. There are five-star hotels, resort hotels, boutique properties, villas, guest homes, and home stays, particularly along the western and southern shores, as well as Kandy and the hill area.

Food in Sri Lanka

How does food taste in Sri Lanka?
It differs from home to home and restaurant to restaurant. But is usually on the slightly spicy side.
What kind of food is available in Sri Lanka?
Rice is served with curries (eggplant, potato, green banana, chicken, and fish) ranging from lightly spiced to near-dynamite. Other delicacies include hoppers, string-hoppers, pittu, lamprais and much more.
What about popular Sri Lankan desserts?
Most common Sri Lankan desserts include Kithul Treacle, Aasmi, Wattalapam, Kalu Dodol,Konda Kavum, Kokis and Helapa.
