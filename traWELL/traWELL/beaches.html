Mount Lavinia

Mount Lavinia, in Colombo's near surroundings, has been noted for its beach since colonial times. With calm and clear water, the sea provides a safe and popular swimming location, and the smooth sandy beach is filled and local families, especially in the evenings.

The town is largely middle-class and residential, and it has yet to see the towering structures that are sprouting up in neighboring cities and metropolitan centers. The beach is almost a mile long and appropriately titled "Golden Mile."

Arugam Bay

Arugam Bay, ranked as the fifth best surf spot in the world, is certainly a paradise on earth. It provides an ideal tropical vacation. If you like to visit Arugam Bay for a holiday, you will find the best surfing conditions, pure white sandy beaches, scarlet sunsets, beachfront guest homes and hotels, and gigantic waves.

Arugam Bay is approximately a 7-hour drive from Colombo. Arugam Bay may be reached by both private and public transportation (buses).

Mirissa's Coast

What was once a little fishing town is gradually becoming a well-known tourist attraction situated against a serene backdrop. Mirissa is really a lovely yet underappreciated village.

Mirissa's Coast, located around 150 kilometers south of Colombo and at an elevation of 4 meters above sea level, is emerging as a magnificent and divine tourism destination. If swimming, snorkeling, diving, and whale watching are on your bucket list, you should drop everything and come to Mirissa right away!
